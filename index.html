About Our Company

Welcome to Hot Air Balloon Tours! We offer a variety of hot air balloon rides over beautiful landscapes. Our experienced pilots and comfortable baskets ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all of our passengers.

Our hot air balloons are equipped with state-of-the-art safety features, and all of our pilots are fully licensed and insured. We take pride in providing a safe and memorable hot air balloon experience for all of our guests.

Tunisian International Balloons Festival

This festival began in 2018 and has attracted over 20 million people since then! It takes place on different parts of Tunisia; however, some of its most popular locations include Djerba, Hammamet, Zaghouan, Nabeul, Tozeur and Douz. 
The event lasts for 3 days each year with 2 days dedicated solely to ballooning activities and 1 day for other activities like shows and other events.
